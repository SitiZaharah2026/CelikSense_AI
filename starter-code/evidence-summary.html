Standards & Methodology
WCAG 2.2 AA Compliant · 15 Pages Bilingual EN / BM Throughout Offline-capable via Service Worker Parental Consent Obtained · Names Anonymised
Methodology: One-week pilot study at an inclusive education programme in Kuala Lumpur, 2026. Three students with documented learning disabilities and one class teacher participated. Parental/guardian consent was obtained for all student participants. All names are anonymised to initials. Sessions were self-directed — students chose which agents to use. Baseline data collected in the preceding week under standard classroom conditions. No financial compensation was provided. Full methodology and raw session logs available on request.
